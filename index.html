Talks by Casey Patrick Driscoll

By day, a full stack web dev of open source technologies that include WordPress and JS at CHIEF.

By night, a dedicated community organizer in the DC Tech community.

Casey has an undergraduate degree in Computer Science from the University of Wisconsin-Eau Claire.

Purpose

Generally speaking, these talks are geared towards 'Aspirational Abstraction', encouraging the listener to think deeper about seemingly simple ideas. No person can fully explain a complex topic in 20 minutes, but hopefully I can pique your curiosity and empower you to explore on your own.
